Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by performing the impossible task of planting an idea into someone’s mind. As Cobb and his team dive through layered dream worlds, the mission becomes increasingly dangerous and personal.

Main Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, and Tom Hardy as Eames. Their performances bring depth and intensity to the complex dream narrative.
